Just a quick tip. Instead of:
margin-top: 1px;
margin-right: 2px;
margin-bottom: 3px;
margin-left: 4px;

You can use:
margin: 1px 2px 3px 4px;

Works for padding too. The order of the inputs is always clockwise (top, right, bottom, left).
